NEHA

Travel is my passion

Neha's journey through Himachal Pradesh's Kangra district has been nothing short of magical. Known for its stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage, Kangra offers picturesque views, historical temples, and vibrant local traditions. Neha often finds herself exploring the beautiful tea gardens, ancient forts, and enjoying the serene environment that Kangra provides.

In Kangra, Neha experiences the perfect blend of nature and history. The district is home to the famous Kangra Fort, Masroor Rock Cut Temple, and the beautiful Dharamshala, which is also the residence of the Dalai Lama. The local cuisine and handicrafts add to the charm of this enchanting district, making Neha's travels truly unforgettable.
