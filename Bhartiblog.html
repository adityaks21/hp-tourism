Bharti

Travel is my passion

My name is Bharti I recently visited Mandi, a serene town in Himachal Pradesh, and it was an unforgettable experience. The tranquility of Prashar Lake, surrounded by the stunning Dhauladhar range, was mesmerizing. Trekking up to the lake was both challenging and rewarding, with breathtaking views along the way.

One of my favorite memories is camping under the starry sky in Barot Valley. The peaceful environment and the sound of the nearby river created a perfect setting for relaxation.Overall, Mandi offered a perfect blend of adventure, spirituality, and natural beauty. It's a destination I would recommend to anyone seeking a rejuvenating escape.
